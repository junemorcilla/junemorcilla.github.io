Fantastic Island

April 2, 2020

A place of fun and great adventure. Funtastic Island is a place to relax and enjoy the ocean breeze that will surely captivate your hearts. The place is worth experiencing with family and friends. The main highlight of Funtastic Island is the cliff jumping that gives you the adrenaline rush and the beautiful cottages on top of the boulders. The view that it offers is really amazing. Going there is already an adventure. Funtastic Island is located at Gibitngil Island, Medellin, so you need to ride a boat to get here.
